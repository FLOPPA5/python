Rotary joints

Catalog.pdf

Rotary joints are high-tech products designed, which is install in rotation nodes for the supply/removal under the pressure of working fluid into the inner cavities of rotating mechanisms. Specialists of our company developed a range of models of rotary joints to different applications, which are protect by patents.
The use of rotary joints increases the life of the rotation nodes and increases the reliability of mechanisms.
The main element of the design of the rotary joints is a pair of end sealing rings, made, as a rule, from a material of increased hardness.
For reliable operation of rotary joints it is necessary to properly install them in the rotation node. All operations must be performed with increased precision and careful.It is not permitted to use for the installation /disassembly a tools of percussions action.
