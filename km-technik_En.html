What we do

Our company develops and manufactures industrial equipment for enterprises of various industries. We started intense activity in the sphere of industrial engineering in 2005. Specialists of our company developed a range
of models of rotary joints for different applications, which are protected by patents.
During this time 50 projects have been realized for enterprises in Ukraine and other countries.
The usage of high-quality components, such as: Lechler nozzles, Elbe Group cardan shafts provides the necessary technical specifications of the equipment. The company consists of project and commercial departments. We have our own production.
